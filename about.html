About Us

Hi My Name is Akshith Vattem a Sophmore at Mountain House High School, me and my partner Rithvik Illuri founded Quick Diagnosis, a digital health companion designed to bridge the gap between you and your health. Our journey began amidst the challenging times of the COVID-19 pandemic, an era that reshaped our perception of healthcare and its accessibility. Witnessing the global health system grappling with unprecedented challenges, we realized the pressing need for solutions that make healthcare advice more accessible, reliable, and timely.
